Professional Experience

Mr. Shamshad Ullah Cheema boasts a robust professional background with over 35 years of experience in legal practice. His expertise spans a broad spectrum of legal areas, including Civil Law, Corporate Law, Real Estate, and Business Law. Mr. Cheema has a distinguished history of representing clients before various legal forums, ranging from District Courts to the Supreme Court of Pakistan. His proficiency extends to handling complex cases in Revenue Matters, Banking Law, Employment/Service/Labour Laws, and Family Laws. Notably, he has demonstrated exceptional skills in Arbitration, Conveyance of Rights in Property, and drafting comprehensive Legal Opinions, Conveyance Deeds, and Agreements. His academic accomplishments are equally impressive, with an LL.M in International Trade Law and an LL.B (Hons.) from the International Islamic University, Islamabad, further solidifying his legal acumen.
